Akan names Generator

Frequently in Ghana, Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. So akan name generator is where you will be required to enter birth day details, and it will automatically calculate and gives you your name in Akan.

Addition on Akan names

The Akan people of Ghana frequently name their children after the day of the week they were born and the order in which they were born. These "day names" have further meanings concerning the soul and character of the person. Middle names have considerably more variety and can refer to their birth order, twin status, or an ancestor's middle name.
